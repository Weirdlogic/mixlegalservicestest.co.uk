Getting Started

How do I request a quote?

Getting a quote from Mix Legal Services is simple:

  1. Visit our contact page or call us directly
  2. Provide basic details about your case
  3. We'll review your requirements and respond within 24 hours
  4. Once you approve the quote, we'll begin work immediately

What information do I need to provide?

For an accurate quote, please provide:

  • Type of case (civil, housing, personal injury, etc.)
  • Court and track allocation
  • Estimated value of the claim
  • Current stage of proceedings
  • Any specific deadlines
  • Whether it's a bill of costs or detailed assessment

Do you work with new clients?

Yes! We welcome new clients and pride ourselves on building long-term relationships. We offer the same high-quality service to all clients, regardless of firm size or case volume.

Services FAQ

What types of cases do you handle?

We handle all types of civil and some criminal matters including:

  • Commercial litigation
  • Personal injury claims
  • Housing disputes
  • Employment cases
  • Family law matters
  • Debt recovery
  • Professional negligence
  • Insolvency proceedings

Do you handle detailed assessments?

Yes, we specialize in detailed assessment work including:

  • Bill of costs preparation
  • Points of dispute responses
  • Supporting documentation
  • Court hearing preparation
  • Settlement negotiations

Can you help with cost budgeting?

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive cost budgeting services including Precedent H preparation, budget variations, and cost management conference support.

Pricing & Payment

How much do your services cost?

Our pricing varies based on case complexity and type:

  • Housing cases: From £120 + VAT
  • Civil matters: From £150 + VAT
  • Detailed assessments: From £200 + VAT
  • Cost analysis: From £180 + VAT
  • Consultations: £150 + VAT per hour

All quotes are provided free of charge and with no obligation.

What are your payment terms?

Our standard payment terms are:

  • 50% deposit required before work commences
  • Balance due upon completion
  • Payment by bank transfer or cheque
  • 30-day payment terms for established clients
  • Payment plans available for larger cases

Do you offer discounts for regular clients?

Yes, we offer competitive rates for regular clients and can discuss volume discounts for firms with high case volumes. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

Our Process

What happens after I accept a quote?

Once you accept our quote:

  1. We'll send you a secure file transfer link
  2. Upload your case files and time records
  3. We'll acknowledge receipt and confirm the timeline
  4. Our team begins work on your bill
  5. We'll contact you if we need any additional information
  6. Quality review by a senior costs lawyer
  7. Final bill delivered via secure email

How do you ensure quality?

Every bill undergoes our rigorous quality process:

  • Initial draft by experienced costs draftsman
  • Compliance check against current court rules
  • Independent review by senior costs lawyer
  • Final accuracy and formatting check
  • Client review period before submission

Document Requirements

What documents do you need?

Typically, we require:

  • Time recording sheets or attendance notes
  • Copy of the court order (if available)
  • Relevant correspondence
  • Fee earner CV/experience details
  • Any previous cost orders in the case
  • Relevant invoices for disbursements

How do I send documents securely?

We provide several secure options:

  • Secure file transfer portal (recommended)
  • Encrypted email attachment
  • Physical delivery for highly sensitive cases
  • Access to your case management system (where possible)

What format should documents be in?

We accept documents in various formats:

  • PDF (preferred for final documents)
  • Word documents
  • Excel spreadsheets
  • Scanned images (JPEG, PNG)
  • Most case management system exports

Turnaround Times

How quickly can you complete work?

Our standard turnaround times are:

  • Simple bills: 1-2 working days
  • Standard civil cases: 2-5 working days
  • Complex commercial matters: 5-10 working days
  • Detailed assessments: 3-7 working days
  • Rush jobs: Same day or next day (surcharge applies)

Can you meet urgent deadlines?

Yes! We understand the pressures of legal practice and offer:

  • Same-day service for urgent cases
  • Weekend and evening work when necessary
  • Priority handling for established clients
  • Clear communication about realistic timescales

Rush work incurs a 50% surcharge on standard fees.
