Services FAQ

What types of cases do you handle?

We handle all types of civil litigation including commercial disputes, personal injury, housing matters, employment cases, family law, and criminal legal aid assessments.

Do you handle both solicitor-client and interparty bills?

Yes, we prepare both solicitor-client bills and interparty bills of costs for all types of proceedings.

Can you help with detailed assessment proceedings?

Absolutely. We can assist with preparing for detailed assessment, including responding to points of dispute and attending hearings if required.

Do you work with legal aid cases?

Yes, we have extensive experience with legal aid assessments and understand the specific requirements for publicly funded cases.

Document Requirements

What We Need From You

To prepare an accurate bill of costs, we typically require:

Essential Documents

  • Time records (detailed breakdown of all work done)
  • Copy of the court order for costs
  • Retainer/client care letter
  • Invoice records and receipts
  • Correspondence files

Case-Specific Documents

  • Counsel's fees notes and invoices
  • Expert witness invoices
  • Court filing fees receipts
  • Travel and accommodation receipts
  • Any other disbursement receipts
