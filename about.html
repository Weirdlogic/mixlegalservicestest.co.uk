Who We Are

Mix Legal Services is a specialized legal cost drafting firm established to help solicitors, barristers, and legal departments maximize their cost recovery. Founded by experienced costs lawyers, we combine deep knowledge of court procedures with practical expertise in cost drafting.

Our team understands the complexities of modern litigation costs and the importance of accurate, compliant cost drafting. We work exclusively in the legal costs field, allowing us to stay current with the latest court rules, practice directions, and case law developments.

To provide exceptional legal cost drafting services that maximize our clients' cost recovery while maintaining the highest standards of accuracy and compliance with court rules.
